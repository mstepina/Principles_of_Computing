"Principles of Computing" is the course offered by Coursera and Rice University, part of the Fundamentals of Computing Specialization.
https://www.coursera.org/learn/principles-of-computing-1/home/welcome
This course introduces the basic mathematical and programming principles that are crucial in creating efficient and well structured solutions for problems in Computer Science.
